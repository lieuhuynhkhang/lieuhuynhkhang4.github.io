TEAM WHITE

The team I decided to work with is named team white. We consist of 4 members: me – Lieu Huynh Khang, Tran Anh Quang, Hoang Duc Minh and Huynh Trung Kien

Personality analysis

Tran Anh Quang

Quang is a very sociable and easy going guy, his test all show remarkably stable figure on all front, all were at around the 50% mark except for agreeableness which he score 85%. This shows that he’s a very cooperative person and that’s a very valuable aspect of teamwork. Personally, I have been friends with Quang for quite some time and work on different projects together. The thing that makes Quang perfect to work with for me is that he’s quite insecure about deadlines. Because of that he’s always prepare early and try to get everyone to divide task and outline the draft very early on, which is one of the most important part for me because my productivity is determine by the amount of time I have left, so I build a bad habit of doing things late. Having someone like Quang on the team force me to give up on that habit and become more efficient with my schedule.

Hoang Duc Minh

Minh is also a friend of mine, he’s a bit more reserve than Quang in terms of extrovert, still, that beats me by a large margin. Minh’s test result also shows very moderate figures except for agreeableness and openness (both around the 70% mark). This implies that like i said Minh is relatively friendly, outgoing and cooperative. It also shows that he’s very creative and open to new ideas, which is also very necessary for IT work. Personally my openness score is 92% with is and is great for IT because the work requires a level of creativity and abstraction understanding, but my neuroticism score is a high 95% which mean im very moody and easily panic, having negative thoughts. Minh on the other hand is a very chill and relaxed guy, being around him help lift my spirit and positively impact not only my but the group’s productivity as a whole.

Huynh Trung Kien

Kien is really a calm and technical person, his demeanor is closest to me in term of quietness. However, once you get to know him, he’s actually a very deep and emotional person. His test result shows that he has a defender type personality, which is described as introverted, observant, feeling, judging, and turbulent. While the test result does shows some of his character, through personal contact I believe the best way to describe Kien is responsible. He’s the kind of person to pay attention to very little detail and try maximize efficiency. Moreover, he always try to complete his duty and doesn’t slack off even there’s temptation. These are very key character in IT Work because coding requires precision and every work (especially working with teams) require responsibility so that your work don’t negatively affects the group. With these regards, Kien is the perfect guy to pull us together when we need to forego our tiredness and push forward.
