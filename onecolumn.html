Contact and sources: Personal Profile

Youtube

Oliver Reiter – System Engineer of Palo Alto Networks

Contact and sources: Youtube

Oliver’s job involves working with clients to improve their cyber security. His daily task includes meeting with businesses, local government and education organizations to identify the challenges they are facing with securing and protecting their data and network environments. Specially a face-to-face meeting to discuss on their requirements then design a solution that can help them (creating a proposal and making sure it fits and integrates into their environment). He does a lot of demonstration, proof of concepts, continuously consulting clients to ensure the solution is working for them and issues are taking care of on the spot. His particular job is a blend between sales and engineering. As such, he spent a lot of his days seeing and interacting with his clients at different locations. He say there are challenges in his work but not as before because when he work in tech support he’s presented with problems every day and it was a challenge to solve them. The challenge in his position now is when working with a big clients like a state organization and his product has problem implementing.

Justin Hammond – Software Engineer

Contact and sources: Youtube

Justin’s work can be a lot of things: working on features, big fixes, scripts, database, internal tools, documentation and so on. His daily task start with checking email about codes and github, then he review codes to make sure the best cod get to production, then he start coding, which also involves troubleshooting. He also have daily meetings with his fellow software engineer to discuss what they are working on, what they are planning to do and what problems are they facing. These can also be trainings, presentation or hanging out. The company he worked for allows for flexible timing so most of his day he work from home, taking care of his kid in the meanwhile and having meeting through video calls. As such, he doesn’t interact with many people besides his colleague IT professional online. His open source project are hosted on github so anyone can contribute to the project. Another crucial part to his work’s success is pair programming where he pair up with another member in the team to watch and learn. This allows ideas to stay fresh, code is well reviewed and learning is convenient.

Jonathan Dalia – IT Manager of Langer Transport Corporation

Contact and sources: Article

Dalia’s job is related to configuring, setting and maintaining all voice, video and data connections at their site. He frequently check backups, server hardware and disaster recovery systems. His daily task also involves troubleshooting incoming issues. When time is available, he would find ways to reduce cost and improve services for enhancing the systems. Through writing report, queries and interacting with the company’s SQL (standard query language) he also manages data. His day would begin with addressing any critical issues first and foremost then moving on to more lower priority ones. After doing so he can turn his attention to ongoing projects. An example on a project is a system which drivers can use to update their orders and locations, built as units installed in trucks. These units are connected back to the database to provide real-time updates. The challenges he faces is troubleshooting remotely and helping users with fast-growing technologies. As he’s the only IT staff in a company that have 3 physical servers and 17 virtual ones, maintenance of all of these will be very difficult. Most of his days is spent at the company’s site for management of these infrastructure. As such, he doesn’t interact with people much since there are not even fellow IT professional working with him, however as an IT manager he does interact with people other departments of the company.
